About the Coffeeshop

Our Coffeeshop is located in the Triple Peaks library. It features 6 tables and free WIFI.

Our mission is keeping guests cozy and energized.

Besides that, we try to reduce out environmental impact - so we don't sell single-use cups. But you can buy one of our resuable cups!
